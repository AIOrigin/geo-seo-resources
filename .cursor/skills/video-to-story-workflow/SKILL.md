# Video To Story Workflow

This skill converts source video material into structured content and story assets.

## Use When

- You have a video and need a storyboard package.
- You want scene breakdown, summary, and derived shot planning.
- You need visual material transformed into reusable story content.

## Core Workflow

1. Extract or organize frames from the source video.
2. Break the input into scenes and summarize the content.
3. Build story structure and reference prompts.
4. Produce shot planning and final story outputs.

## Typical Outputs

- Scene breakdown
- Content summary
- Story structure
- Reference prompts and shot plans
- Final story text
